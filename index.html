ABOUT

I graduated from Georgia Tech in December 2024 with a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Computing & Intelligence (AI/ML). Currently, I am working full-time at SpaceX as a Tower and Mount Engineer and part-time on my startup MyField. Previously, I worked in the Nonlinear Mechanics Lab supervised by Dr. Michael Leamy, and I also participated in the Advanced SLA Printing Lab co-supervised by Dr. Amit Jariwala and Dr. Robert Schwerzel. I was also the BattleBots Project Manager in RoboJackets and Build Director in Science Olympiad @ GT.
